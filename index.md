Welcome! I am a Ph.D. student in the Department of Political Science at the [University of Massachusetts Amherst](https://www.umass.edu/political-science/).

My research examines how the intersection of political identities influences political behavior and policy preferences. I am broadly interested in race and ethnic politics and the politics of immigration. In my dissertation project, I examine how the transition to parenthood and the development of parental identity influence policy preferences. I am also interested in designing survey experiments and applying statistical methods to measure identity salience and how policy attitudes change in response to external threats.

I can be reached at my email: seungjoonyoo [at] umass [dot] edu.
